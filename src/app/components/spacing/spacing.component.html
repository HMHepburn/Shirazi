Spacing

Spacing is important to give elements room to breathe on a website page. Spacing creates a hierarchy of information that relays importance to the user.

Law of Proximity

1. Related elements should be spaced closer together. Conversely, unrelated elements should be spaced further apart.

2. Elements of the same “type” should be spaced evenly apart.

Point Grids

The 8pt grid is used in most design to space elements. Some multiples of 4pt are also acceptable.

It is best practise to use rem instead of pixels due to its responsiveness.
The conversion rate between pixels and rem is 16px = 0.5rem

Conclusions

Spacing is a practise of intuition - most of the time it's best to use a spacing that you feel looks and feels best.
