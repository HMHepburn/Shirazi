Colours

The colours we choose on our site reflect the brand image to users.
The colours used for components may change depending on whether we are using a dark or light theme.

Colours should display elements with sufficient contrast. WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) describe a ratio of 4.5:1 for saturation of text against dark surfaces. This means we should use unsaturated colours in dark themes, and saturated colours for light themes.

This is a useful site for checking whether colour contrast is accessible.
