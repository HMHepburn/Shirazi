Layout

Cards may be arranged only in grid form. Ideally they should each be the same size and shape.
Groups of cards should have be homogeneous regardless of content within the cards.
Spacing between cards should also be homogeneous, and the card layout should ideally fill the viewport

DO: Equal spacing and sizing between cards

DON'T: Unequal sizing and awkward spacing
